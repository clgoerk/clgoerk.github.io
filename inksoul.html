InkSoul

Tattoo & Piercing Studio

InkSoul is a professional tattoo and piercing studio dedicated to turning your ideas into lasting art. With a creative and welcoming atmosphere, clients can explore unique tattoo designs, professional piercings, and personalized consultations to ensure every piece reflects their individuality. From first-time clients to seasoned collectors, InkSoul makes the experience of body art safe, inspiring, and unforgettable.

×

Key Features

  • Home Page – Highlights core services and rotating Flash Sale tattoos with pricing and availability.
  • Gallery – Showcases completed work with category filters (style, body part, artist, etc.) and detailed captions.
  • Artists – Dedicated profiles with bio, specialties, and an auto-filtered portfolio of pieces created by each artist.
  • Services – Clear breakdown of tattoo and piercing services, aftercare, and step-by-step studio process.
  • Booking – Simple form to request consultations, piercings, or tattoos; routes to the backend for scheduling and confirmations.
  • Admin Dashboard – Manage appointments, maintain artist portfolios, upload gallery images (with descriptions and artist attribution), and update Flash Sale items.
  • Real-Time Updates – All admin actions write to the backend and instantly reflect on the public site.

Technologies Used:

  • PHP & MySQL for managing artists, appointments, and gallery content.
  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript for responsive, modern web design.
  • Custom Admin Dashboard to handle bookings, artist profiles, and gallery updates.
