About Me

Hello! I’m a passionate student at triOS College, where I’m diving deep into the world of mobile and web development. With a strong focus on creating user-friendly and innovative solutions, I’m honing my skills in both front-end and back-end technologies. My coursework and projects have equipped me with a solid foundation in programming languages, frameworks, and design principles.

As I continue to learn and grow, I’m eager to apply my knowledge to real-world challenges and contribute to exciting projects. Whether it’s developing sleek mobile applications or building dynamic websites, I’m driven by a desire to create impactful and efficient solutions. Feel free to explore my portfolio and get in touch if you’d like to collaborate or learn more about my work!

Career Goals

  • Master Core Development Skills
    • Build a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other web technologies, and continuously improve my programming skills in both frontend and backend development. Aim to master frameworks such as React for web and Flutter or Swift for mobile development.
  • Create a Strong Portfolio
    • Develop a professional portfolio that showcases a variety of projects, from responsive websites to functional mobile applications, to demonstrate my technical skills, creativity, and growth as a developer.
  • Contribute to Open Source Projects
    • Actively participate in open-source projects to gain hands-on experience, learn from industry peers, and give back to the development community. This involvement will help me understand industry standards and collaborate with other developers.
  • Build Real-World Projects
    • Create and release real-world projects and applications, aiming to publish at least one mobile app and one web application by the end of my studies. These projects will serve as practical examples of my problem-solving abilities and user-centered design approach.
  • Focus on UI/UX Design
    • Hone skills in user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) design to create applications that are both visually appealing and highly functional. This includes learning design tools like Figma and Sketch and studying UX principles to better understand user needs.
  • Develop Strong Collaboration Skills
    • Work on team projects to develop effective communication, problem-solving, and project management skills. Collaborating with others will enhance my ability to work well in a professional environment.
  • Stay Updated with Industry Trends
    • Keep learning and adapting to emerging trends and technologies in mobile and web development, such as AI integration, progressive web apps (PWAs), and serverless architecture. Continuous learning will be essential for my growth and relevance in the field.
  • Prepare for Professional Certification
    • Aim to earn relevant certifications, such as in front-end development or mobile app development, to validate my knowledge and make myself more competitive for internships and entry-level positions.
  • Secure an Internship or Entry-Level Role
    • Seek an internship or entry-level role with a company where I can apply my skills, gain practical experience, and learn from industry experts. Real-world experience will be invaluable as I transition from student to professional.
  • Build a Professional Network
    • Connect with other developers, mentors, and industry professionals through platforms like LinkedIn, GitHub, and developer conferences. Building a strong network will open doors for future opportunities and provide support as I progress in my career.
